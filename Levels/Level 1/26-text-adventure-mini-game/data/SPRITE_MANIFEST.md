## Notes

- ✓ = Sprite file exists
- ✗ = Placeholder (file missing)
- Sprites are automatically loaded from `assets/sprites/` (recursive) and `assets/tilesets/default/`
- Missing sprites use `AssetManager._make_placeholder()` which generates colored shapes
- Priority levels: critical > high > medium > low
